Classic Salkantay + Inca Trail – Trekking to Machu Picchu

Trekking through the Salkantay mountain is connecting with living nature surrounded by lakes, rivers, flora and fauna and with a lot of history because they were sacred places and paths of the Incas which they used to perform religious ceremonies to the apus (which means god of the mountain), which are the ones that protected them and allowed the Incas to get closer to their important god who was the sun, that is, the higher the mountains were, the more important they were for the Incas.
